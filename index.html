System Architecture & Capabilities

Gesture → Speech

Distributed neckband and wearable sensing capture spatial hand trajectories and facial markers, translating gestures into natural spoken audio in real time.

Speech → Avatar

Incoming speech is processed on-chip to render an expressive, localized 3D signing avatar for clear reciprocal conversation.

Privacy & Resilience

Built for low-resource environments. Local inference means complete data privacy and zero dependence on continuous internet coverage.

Multimodal Fusion

Computer vision, inertial spatial tracking, and NPU acceleration combine to deliver accurate recognition under dynamic real-world lighting.
