Bidirectional African Sign Language Translation, on the Edge

Aperture Signs is pioneering low-latency, assistive AI technology designed for seamless, bidirectional communication between deaf individuals and non-signers in low-resource environments — no cloud dependency, no compromise on privacy.

Gesture → Speech

Wearable sensing captures hand and facial motion, converting it instantly into natural spoken language.

Speech → Avatar

Spoken language is rendered back as an expressive, localized signing avatar for immediate feedback.

Built for Africa

Kenyan Sign Language first, with a model architecture designed to extend across regional languages.
